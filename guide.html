Cash Cleaner Strategy Guide – How to Clean Every Bill Perfectly

How to Play

Cash Cleaner is a relaxing, stress-relief experience: satisfying cleaning with smooth motion and crisp feedback. Follow these steps for visual calm and maximum satisfaction—no rush, just methodical, ASMR-style play.

  1. Use the Eraser for light dust. Pick up a dirty bill from the tray or money bag. Switch to the eraser (or click the 3D eraser on the table) and rub over dusty areas—watch the dirt melt away with satisfying, smooth motion.
  1. Use the Spray Cleaner for stubborn oil stains. Some spots won’t come off with the eraser—they need the spray. Equip the spray cleaner and apply it to oily or dark stains; the relaxing dissolve effect gives crisp feedback until the bill is fully clean.
  1. Check with UV Light for counterfeit bills. After a bill looks clean, hold it under the desk UV lamp. Real bills show a fluorescent strip; fakes do not. You’ll get clear, satisfying feedback—then collect (real) or destroy/keep (fake).
  2. Stack your cash and build your empire. Verified real bills stack with satisfying visual calm; fakes can’t be collected. Keep cleaning, verifying, and stacking to grow your score and unlock shop upgrades.

Tools Explained

When to use Eraser vs Spray: The eraser removes light dust and dry dirt with smooth, satisfying motion. If a stain doesn’t budge after a few passes, it’s usually an oil-type stain—switch to the Spray Cleaner. Use the spray on dark or greasy spots; once they melt away, finish any remaining dust with the eraser for that crisp feedback.

How UV Light helps detect fake bills: Under the UV lamp, genuine bills display a bright fluorescent strip (security feature). Counterfeit bills do not, so the game marks them as fake. Always check under UV before stacking so you don’t waste time on bills that won’t count toward your score—keeping your session stress-free and focused.

Pro Tips

  • Clean faster: Work in order—erase first, then spray only where needed. The satisfying rhythm keeps the experience relaxing without spraying the whole bill when it’s mostly dusty.
  • Avoid missing stains: Sweep the eraser or spray over the entire surface with smooth motion. Dark spots that don’t fade need the spray—crisp feedback tells you when you’re done.
  • Earn money more efficiently: Focus on cleaning and UV-checking in a steady flow; use shop upgrades (eraser range, solvent range, fake reduction) when you have enough cash for a smoother, more satisfying run.
