Cash Cleaner Strategy Guide – How to Clean Every Bill Perfectly

How to Play

  1. Use the Eraser for light dust. Pick up a dirty bill from the tray or money bag. Switch to the eraser (or click the 3D eraser on the table) and rub over dusty areas until they’re gone.
  2. Use the Spray Cleaner for stubborn oil stains. Some spots won’t come off with the eraser—they need the spray. Equip the spray cleaner and apply it to oily or dark stains until the bill is fully clean.
  3. Check with UV Light for counterfeit bills. After a bill looks clean, hold it under the desk UV lamp. Real bills show a fluorescent strip; fakes do not. You’ll be prompted to collect (real) or destroy/keep (fake).
  4. Stack your cash and build your empire. Verified real bills are stacked; fakes can’t be collected. Keep cleaning, verifying, and stacking to grow your score and unlock shop upgrades.

Tools Explained

When to use Eraser vs Spray: The eraser removes light dust and dry dirt. If a stain doesn’t budge after a few passes, it’s usually an oil-type stain—switch to the Spray Cleaner. Use the spray on dark or greasy spots; once they’re gone, you can finish any remaining dust with the eraser.

How UV Light helps detect fake bills: Under the UV lamp, genuine bills display a bright fluorescent strip (security feature). Counterfeit bills do not, so the game marks them as fake. Always check under UV before stacking so you don’t waste time on bills that won’t count toward your score.

Pro Tips

  • Clean faster: Work in order—erase first, then spray only where needed. Don’t spray the whole bill if most of it is just dusty.
  • Avoid missing stains: Rotate or zoom the bill if you can, and sweep the eraser/spray over the entire surface. Dark spots that don’t fade need the spray.
  • Earn money more efficiently: Focus on cleaning and UV-checking quickly; use shop upgrades (e.g. eraser range, solvent range, fake reduction) when you have enough cash to speed up later runs.
